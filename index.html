About James

A passion for music,
a gift for teaching.

Hi, I'm James — a guitarist and music tutor with years of experience helping students of all ages find their voice on the guitar. Whether you're drawn to acoustic fingerpicking, electric rock, or everything in between, I tailor every lesson to you.

I believe that learning an instrument should be enjoyable from the very first lesson. My approach is patient, structured, and always guided by what you want to play.

  • ♫ All styles: rock, acoustic, blues, classical, pop
  • ♫ Ages 7 and up — beginners to advanced
  • ♫ Online & in-person lessons available
  • ♫ Grade exam preparation (ABRSM & RockSchool)
